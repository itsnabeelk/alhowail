Al Howail Group operates in a range of industries such as construction, engineering, trading, manufacturing, and real estate. The company was established in 1969 and has since grown into a diversified conglomerate with a wide range of activities. Al Howail Group has completed several notable projects in Saudi Arabia, including the construction of the King Faisal Specialist Hospital in Riyadh and the expansion of the Jeddah Islamic Port. The company is also involved in real estate development, and it has developed several residential and commercial properties in Riyadh and other parts of Saudi Arabia.
